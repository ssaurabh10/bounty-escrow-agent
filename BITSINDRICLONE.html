About BIT Sindri

Established in 1949, BIT Sindri is one of India's oldest government engineering and technological institutes. BIT Sindri is known for its contribution to the field of higher technical education and research. We aim to provide valuable human resources for industry and society through excellence in technical education and research for sustainable development.
